About the Author

Hadiza Bagudu is a Nigerian author and poet whose works explore the intricate tapestry of human relationships against the backdrop of African culture and society.

With "The Thin Line," she weaves a compelling narrative that resonates with readers across cultures, examining universal themes through a distinctly Nigerian lens.

Her writing has been praised for its emotional depth, vivid characterization, and ability to capture the complexities of modern African life.
